About Us

Welcome to TicketSwap, your trusted platform for buying and selling second-hand tickets. We’re here to make your event experiences seamless, affordable, and hassle-free.

Who We Are

We are Subhan Siddiqui and Abubakr Ansari, two passionate B.Tech Computer Science students with a shared vision of revolutionizing the way people buy and sell tickets. As engineering students, we’ve combined our technical expertise and love for problem-solving to create a platform that connects people and makes ticket exchange easier than ever.

Our Story

The idea for TicketSwap was born out of our own struggles with buying and selling tickets for concerts, sports events, and train journeys. We noticed how difficult it was to find reliable, affordable, and trustworthy platforms for second-hand tickets. Determined to solve this problem, we decided to create a peer-to-peer (P2P) platform that empowers users to buy and sell tickets securely and efficiently.

What We Do

At TicketSwap, we specialize in connecting buyers and sellers of second-hand tickets for:

  • Concerts – Never miss your favorite artist’s performance!
  • Sports Events – Cheer for your team live at the stadium.
  • Train Tickets – Find last-minute travel options or sell unused tickets.

Our platform ensures a safe, transparent, and user-friendly experience for everyone.

Why Choose Us?

  • Trustworthy: We prioritize safety and authenticity. Every ticket listed on our platform is verified to ensure legitimacy.
  • Affordable: Say goodbye to overpriced tickets! Our P2P model allows users to buy and sell tickets at fair prices.
  • Easy to Use: Our platform is designed with simplicity in mind, making it easy for anyone to navigate and use.
  • Eco-Friendly: By promoting the reuse of tickets, we’re reducing waste and contributing to a more sustainable future.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to create a seamless and secure platform where ticket enthusiasts can connect, trade, and enjoy unforgettable experiences. We believe that everyone deserves the chance to attend their favorite events without breaking the bank.

Join Us on This Journey

Whether you’re looking to buy a ticket to your dream concert or sell an unused ticket, TicketSwap is here for you. Together, let’s make ticket exchange easier, safer, and more enjoyable.

Thank you for being a part of our story. Let’s swap tickets and create memories!
