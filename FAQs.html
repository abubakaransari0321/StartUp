Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy tickets on TicketSwap?

To buy tickets, simply search for the event you’re interested in, select the ticket you want, and proceed to checkout. You’ll receive your ticket via email after payment is confirmed.

Is it safe to buy second-hand tickets?

Yes! We verify every ticket listed on our platform to ensure authenticity. If you encounter any issues, our support team is here to help.

How do I sell my tickets?

To sell tickets, create an account, list your ticket with the event details, and set your price. Once sold, you’ll receive payment after the event.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major UPIs,credit/debit cards, Net Banking and other secure payment methods.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at support@ticketswap.com or through our contact form. We’re here to help!
